Basketball Rules

Basketball Rules

  1. There are two teams, each with five players on the court.


    2.
  2. The game is played with a ball and two baskets, one at each end of the court.


    3.
  3. The objective is to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent's basket while preventing the opponent from doing the same.


    4.
  4. A player can move the ball by dribbling or passing it to a teammate.


    5.
  5. A player cannot run with the ball, but can take up to two steps before dribbling or passing.


    6.
  6. If a player violates a rule, such as traveling or double dribbling, the ball is turned over to the other team.


    7.
  7. The game is divided into four quarters, each lasting 12 minutes at the professional level.


    8.
  8. Players can foul each other to prevent them from scoring, but excessive or dangerous fouls can result in ejection from the game.


    9.
  9. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.
